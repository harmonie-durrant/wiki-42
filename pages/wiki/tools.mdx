## Tools - List [#tools-list]

### [Grademe](https://grademe.fr/) [#grademe]

Grademe is a tool that simulates the 42 Exam environment. It allows you to practice your exam skills and get instant feedback on your performance.


<div className="mockup-code mt-4">

### [XP Calculator](https://42.tbailleu.dev/) [#xp-calculator]

The XP Calculator is a tool that helps you calculate the number of xp you will need to get to the next level.
